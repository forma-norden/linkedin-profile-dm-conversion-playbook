# LinkedIn Benchmarks

Performance data for LinkedIn organic growth, content reach, and DM pipeline.

## Content Reach Multipliers

Baseline (1.0x) = A standard text + image post.

| Format | Reach Multiplier | Effort | Use Case |
|--------|------------------|--------|----------|
| PDF Carousel | 2.5x - 3.5x | High | Deep education, frameworks, how-to |
| Text-only | 1.2x - 1.5x | Low | Contrarian takes, stories |
| Text + Image (Selfie) | 1.5x - 2.0x | Low | Personal branding, authenticity |
| Text + Image (Graphic) | 1.0x | Medium | Standard announcements |
| Video (Native) | 0.8x - 1.2x | High | Brand building, trust (low reach, high trust) |
| Link in Post | 0.4x - 0.6x | Low | External promotion (Avoid when possible) |

## Engagement Impact (The "Golden Hour")

Engagement within the first 60 minutes dictates 80% of total post reach.

| Action | Relative Weight | Note |
|--------|-----------------|------|
| Comment (>15 words) | 4x | Highly rewarded by the algorithm |
| Repost with Thoughts | 3x | Good, but often gets less organic reach than a comment |
| Short Comment | 1.5x | "Great post!" (Minimal algorithm impact) |
| Like / React | 1x | Baseline engagement signal |

## Connection Acceptance Rates

| Approach | Acceptance Rate | Context |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| Blank invite (good profile) | 25-35% | Let your headline do the work |
| Relevant personalized note | 30-40% | "Saw your comment on X..." |
| Generic personalized note | 10-15% | "I help companies like yours..." (Avoid) |
| Warm (after they engaged) | 40-60%+ | Connecting with commenters |

## DM Conversion Rates

| Cohort | Reply Rate | Call Booking Rate |
|--------|------------|-------------------|
| Cold (no prior engagement) | 5-10% | < 1% |
| Warm (engaged with your post) | 25-40% | 5-8% |
| Lead Magnet Delivery | 80%+ | 10-15% (if friction is low) |

## Profile Optimization

| Element | Benchmark |
|---------|-----------|
| Profile Photo | Clear, contrasting background, smiling |
| Banner | Clear value prop, social proof, clean design |
| Headline | [Authority Title] \| [Who I Help & How] \| [Proof] |
| Featured Section | 2-3 items max (opt-in, case study, calendar) |
| About Section | Formatted for readability, clear CTA at bottom |

## Growth Cadence

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Posting Frequency | 3-5x per week |
| Commenting (Outbound) | 10-20 meaningful comments/day on ICP and peer posts |
| Connection Requests | 15-20/day targeted (avoid automated limits) |
| Consistency Horizon | 6 months (minimum to see compounding pipeline) |
